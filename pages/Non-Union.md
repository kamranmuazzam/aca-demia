- #definition {{c1 When fracture is not united clinically or radiologically within 9 months}} #ortho-prof-card
- ## Etiology
  background-color:: blue
	- ## Factors Responsible for Non-Union #ortho-prof-card #ortho-prof-written 
	  Causes
		- ### **I. Local Factors**
			- #### **1. Mechanical Factors**
				- **Excessive motion at fracture site** (poor fixation, inadequate immobilization)
				- **Gap at fracture site** (due to soft tissue interposition, distraction, or bone loss)
				- **Poor reduction** (malalignment leading to instability)
			- #### **2. Biological Factors**
				- **Poor blood supply** (due to severe soft tissue injury, high-energy trauma, or vascular compromise)
				- **Infection** (osteomyelitis impairs healing)
				- **Bone loss** (critical-sized defect prevents bridging)
				- **Periosteal stripping** (compromises osteogenic cells)
			- #### **3. Fracture-Specific Factors**
				- **Location of fracture** (e.g., scaphoid, talus, tibia due to poor vascularity)
				- **Severe comminution** (reduces contact area for healing)
				- **Open fractures** (higher risk of infection and soft tissue damage)
		- ### **II. Systemic Factors**
			- #### **1. Metabolic & Nutritional Factors**
				- **Vitamin D deficiency** (impairs mineralization)
				- **Calcium & phosphorus imbalance**
				- **Protein malnutrition** (delays callus formation)
				- **Diabetes mellitus** (microangiopathy, poor healing)
			- #### **2. Hormonal Factors**
				- **Hypothyroidism** (slows bone turnover)
				- **Growth hormone deficiency** (reduces osteoblast activity)
			- #### **3. Medications & Substances**
				- **NSAIDs** (inhibit prostaglandins, reducing bone formation)
				- **Corticosteroids** (suppress osteoblast activity)
				- **Chemotherapy/Radiation** (impair cellular proliferation)
				- **Smoking & Alcohol** (vasoconstriction, reduced osteogenesis)
			- #### **4. Other Systemic Conditions**
				- **Anemia** (reduces oxygen delivery to fracture site)
				- **Chronic diseases** (e.g., renal failure, rheumatoid arthritis)
