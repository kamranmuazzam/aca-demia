- ## Prognosis
  background-color:: blue
	- ### Ranson Score
	  A score of 3 or above indicates severity
		- #### On Admission
			- Age > 55 years
			- White Blood Cell Count > $$16 \times 10^9 /L$$
			- Blood Glucose ≥ 11.1 mmol/L
			- LDH > 350 units/L
			- AST > 250 units/L
		- Within 48 hours
			- Haemotocrit fall of 10% or greater
			- BUN rise > 5 mg/dL (1.8 mmol/L) despite fluids
			- Arterial Oxygen Saturation $$\ce{PaO2}$$ < 60 mm Hg
			- Serum Calcium <8 mg/dL
			- Base deficit > 4 mmol/L
			- Fluid sequestration > 6 liters
	- ### Expected Course
